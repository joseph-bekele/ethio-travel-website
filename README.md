A modern, interactive travel booking website showcasing Ethiopia's most beautiful and culturally significant destinations.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection (for embedded maps and fonts)

### Installation
1. Clone or download this repository
2. Extract files to your desired location
3. No server or installation required!

### Usage
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Browse destinations and click "View Details" for more information
3. Select a hotel from the Hotels section or click "Book Hotel" on a destination card
4. Fill in your booking details in the Booking form
5. Review the automatic price calculation
6. Click "Complete Booking" to finalize your reservation

## 🌐 Destinations Information

### Lalibela
- **Distance**: 638 km (9-10 hours)
- **Languages**: Amharic, Agaw
- **Altitude**: 2,600 m
- **Best Time**: October to February
- **Highlights**: 11 rock-hewn churches, UNESCO World Heritage site

### Simien Mountains
- **Distance**: 330 km (5-6 hours)
- **Languages**: Amharic, Tigrinya
- **Altitude**: 4,550 m
- **Best Time**: October to February
- **Highlights**: Trekking, Endemic wildlife, Scenic views

### Axum
- **Distance**: 555 km (8-9 hours)
- **Languages**: Tigrinya, Amharic
- **Altitude**: 2,131 m
- **Best Time**: October to February
- **Highlights**: Ancient stelae, Historical sites, Archaeology

### Danakil Depression
- **Distance**: 500 km (8-9 hours)
- **Languages**: Afar
- **Altitude**: -116 m (below sea level)
- **Best Time**: November to February
- **Highlights**: Geothermal features, Salt mining, Volcanoes

### Awash National Park
- **Distance**: 225 km (3-4 hours)
- **Languages**: Oromo, Amharic
- **Altitude**: 1,500-2,400 m
- **Best Time**: October to February
- **Highlights**: Safari, Waterfall, Wildlife

### Lake Tana
- **Distance**: 305 km (5-6 hours)
- **Languages**: Amharic, Agaw
- **Altitude**: 1,830 m
- **Best Time**: October to February
- **Highlights**: Island monasteries, Blue Nile Falls, Boat tours
