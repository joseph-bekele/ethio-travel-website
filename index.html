Featured Destinations

Lalibela

Distance from Addis Ababa: 638 km (9-10 hours)

Languages: Amharic, Agaw

Altitude: 2,600 m

Home to the famous rock-hewn churches dating back to the 12th century. A UNESCO World Heritage site and one of Ethiopia's most sacred pilgrimage destinations.

🏛️ Historical ⛪ Religious 📸 Scenic

Simien Mountains

Distance from Addis Ababa: 330 km (5-6 hours)

Languages: Amharic, Tigrinya

Altitude: 4,550 m

A dramatic mountain range with stunning escarpments, deep valleys, and rare wildlife including the Ethiopian wolf and Gelada baboons.

🏔️ Trekking 🦁 Wildlife 🌄 Nature

Axum (Aksum)

Distance from Addis Ababa: 555 km (8-9 hours)

Languages: Tigrinya, Amharic

Altitude: 2,131 m

Ancient capital of the Aksumite Empire featuring massive stone stelae, historical artifacts, and the Church of St. Mary of Zion.

⛩️ Ancient 🏺 Archaeology 🌟 Cultural

Danakil Depression

Distance from Addis Ababa: 500 km (8-9 hours)

Languages: Afar

Altitude: -116 m (below sea level)

One of the hottest and most inhospitable places on Earth, featuring colorful geothermal features, salt flats, and otherworldly landscapes.

🌋 Geothermal 🌅 Unique 🚴 Adventure

Awash National Park

Distance from Addis Ababa: 225 km (3-4 hours)

Languages: Oromo, Amharic

Altitude: 1,500-2,400 m

Home to diverse wildlife including lions, giraffes, and gazelles. Features the scenic Awash waterfall and volcanic landscapes.

🦁 Safari 💧 Waterfalls 🌳 Nature

Lake Tana

Distance from Addis Ababa: 305 km (5-6 hours)

Languages: Amharic, Agaw

Altitude: 1,830 m

Ethiopia's largest lake with numerous islands containing ancient monasteries, beautiful landscapes, and the Blue Nile Falls.

⛪ Monasteries 🚤 Cruising 📸 Photography
